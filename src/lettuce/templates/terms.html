5. Privacy & Data Governance

Lettuce retains Customer Data for the duration that the Bot is installed and active in your Slack workspace. Specifically:

Data Archival & Removal Policy

Lettuce will remove Customer Data in accordance with the following:

6. Slack Integration

The Bot operates within Slack's ecosystem and is subject to:

  • Slack's Terms of Service
  • Slack's Privacy Policy
  • Slack's Community Code of Conduct

The Bot requests the following permissions:

  • app_mentions:read – Detect mentions for context-aware engagement
  • chat:write – Send messages to channels and direct messages
  • chat:write.customize – Add branding to messages
  • commands – Handle slash commands
  • users:read – Access user information for personalization
  • team:read – Access workspace information
  • All message history scopes – Read conversation history for context
